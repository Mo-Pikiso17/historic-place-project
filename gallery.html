    The four royals

    The four monuments are of prisoners that were once held at Caslte of Good Hope during the Boer War regime. They were held in cells due to their resistance against land disposession imposed by by the Dutch settlers. The four royals are: King Cetshwayo, King Langalibalele, King Sekhukhune and resistance leader Doman.

    Key Ceremony
